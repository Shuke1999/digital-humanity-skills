# DH Venues Guide

## Primary Journals

### Digital Humanities Quarterly (DHQ)
- **Publisher:** ADHO (Alliance of Digital Humanities Organizations), open access
- **URL:** https://www.digitalhumanities.org/dhq/
- **Scope:** Broad DH — theory, method, praxis; welcomes hybrid technical+interpretive work
- **Citation style:** Chicago or MLA
- **Review type:** Peer-reviewed, open review process
- **Acceptance:** Relatively selective; long-form essays preferred
- **Indexed:** Scopus (2024), Clarivate Q1

### Digital Scholarship in the Humanities (DSH / formerly LLC)
- **Publisher:** Oxford University Press on behalf of ADHO
- **Scope:** Computational approaches to humanities; corpus linguistics, stylometry, text analysis
- **Citation style:** Oxford Notes system
- **Review type:** Double-blind peer review
- **Acceptance:** Competitive; tends toward quantitative methods

### Journal of Cultural Analytics
- **URL:** https://culturalanalytics.org/
- **Scope:** Quantitative and computational approaches to cultural phenomena; literary studies, history, sociology of culture
- **Style:** APA or Chicago Author-Date
- **Open access:** Yes
- **Notable papers:** Moretti-lineage distant reading; corpus-scale literary analysis

### Digital Humanities Kommunity (DHK) / DH Commons
- Open-access forum for shorter, experimental DH work

## Key Conferences

### ADHO Annual Conference (DH20xx)
- The flagship international DH conference
- DH2023: Graz, Austria — 250 papers, 77 sessions
- DH2024: Arlington VA (Roy Rosenzweig Center) — theme: Reinvention and Responsibility
- Abstracts typically 300–500 words; proceedings indexed in DH Abstracts Library (UVA)
- **URL:** https://adho.org/

### Digital Humanities Conference (DHC)
- UK-based; organized by the Digital Humanities Institute, Sheffield
- More humanistically-oriented than ADHO

### ACL / EMNLP DH Workshops
- Computational linguistics venues with DH tracks
- Better for NLP-heavy DH work (NER, parsing historical text, OCR correction)

### CHI (ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems)
- Relevant for DH tools, interfaces, and archival systems work

## Choosing the Right Venue

| If your work is... | Submit to... |
|----|-----|
| Broad DH theory or mixed methods | DHQ |
| Quantitative corpus analysis | DSH or Cultural Analytics |
| Cultural/literary computing | Cultural Analytics or DSH |
| NLP applied to historical text | ACL DH workshops, JDMDH |
| DH tools / interfaces | CHI, CSCW |
| Exploratory / experimental | DH Commons, DH conference |

## Open-Access Preprint Options
- **arXiv cs.CL, cs.IR**: For computational DH with NLP methods
- **Humanities Commons CORE**: Humanistic DH
- **Zenodo**: Datasets and software alongside papers
