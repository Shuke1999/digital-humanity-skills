The key points for dh-explore agents:

1. **Always determine methodology orientation during intake** — ask the user where on the computational ↔ interpretive spectrum they intend to work
2. **OCR quality gates the methodology** — if corpus accuracy is below 70%, steer toward keyword/frequency methods rather than NER or topic modeling
3. **The historical-agent use case** (this repo) typically sits at the hybrid position — computational NLP over noisy historical text, with humanistic interpretation of findings
4. **Franco Moretti's distant/close reading framework** is the canonical framing for introducing this spectrum to new DH researchers
